Pokémon Collection

My collection of Pokémon cards

My Pokémon card collection started when I was young, with the classic base set cards. Unfortunately, my mother threw out all of my Pokemon cards, not knowing the gold mine that we had in our basement! Recently, I've been getting back into the hobby and it's been a lot of fun. Opening packs with friends and chasing after the rarer cards has been an extremely satisfying experience. Here are some of my favorite cards from my collection!
