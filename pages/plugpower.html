Plug Power Experience

My contributions to clean energy technology and hydrogen fuel cell systems

During my time at Plug Power, I had the opportunity to work at the forefront of clean energy technology, specifically in hydrogen fuel cell systems. As an Embedded Systems Engineer, I was part of a dedicated team focused on developing and maintaining critical components of Plug Power's fuel cell infrastructure. My role involved working with various embedded systems, from the Operators Interface to the Cell Voltage Monitor and System Safety Board, where I implemented robust solutions using C/C# and PCAN protocols.

The experience was particularly rewarding as I contributed to both hardware and software aspects of the systems. I developed internal tools that streamlined development processes, created comprehensive testing applications, and implemented safety-critical firmware updates. One of my most significant contributions was optimizing the build process for PIC18F chipsets, which dramatically improved firmware development efficiency. Additionally, I had the opportunity to work on innovative projects, such as leveraging AI models to develop a database frontend that bridged the gap between controls and software engineering teams.
